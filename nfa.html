NFA LAWS OF THE GAME
1. SEASON PHASES

Each season spans 6 months and is divided into League, Continental, and World phases. Consistency across all phases determines the overall Nexus Legend status.

2. FINANCIAL FAIR PLAY (FFP)

Clubs are strictly prohibited from spending beyond their 800M Credit grant. Fines, transfer bans, or point deductions will be applied to violating franchises.

3. PLAYER INACTIVITY

Players inactive for 45 days enter the Watchlist. At 75 days, the NFA reserves the right to terminate contracts and release players into Free Agency.
