RULES & REGULATIONS

To ensure a fair and professional arena for all strikers, the following rules are strictly enforced.

ZERO TOLERANCE: Use of any third-party scripts, network manipulation (lagging), or toxic behavior will result in a permanent ban.

1. MATCHPLAY RULES

Match Duration: All tournament matches follow the standard 10-minute duration unless specified otherwise.
Disconnections: If a player disconnects before the 70th minute, the match must be restarted. After the 70th minute, the current score stands.

2. REPORTING SCORES

  • Both players must take a screenshot of the final score screen.
  • Results must be posted in the designated WhatsApp/Discord channel within 15 minutes of completion.

TERMS & CONDITIONS

By entering the Nexus Legends arena, you agree to the following legal terms.

1. ELIGIBILITY

Players must be at least 13 years of age. One account per player is allowed. Account sharing during tournaments is strictly prohibited.

2. PRIZE DISTRIBUTION

Prize pools are distributed via the method stated in the tournament roster within 7 working days after the grand final. Any attempt to provide false payment details will result in prize forfeiture.

3. CONTENT USAGE

Nexus Legends reserves the right to stream, record, and use player names/team logos for promotional purposes across our digital platforms.

PRIVACY POLICY

Your data security is paramount in our arena.

1. DATA COLLECTION

We collect minimal data required for tournament organization:

  • In-game Name (IGN)
  • Contact Number (WhatsApp for coordination)
  • Tournament Performance Stats

2. DATA PROTECTION

We do not sell your data to third parties. Your contact information is only visible to Arena Admins for the purpose of match coordination and prize payouts.

3. COOKIES

Our website uses local storage to keep you logged into the Admin Dashboard (if applicable) and to save your site preferences.
